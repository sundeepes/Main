L2 - AWS Administrator/DevOps Engineer
Job Title: AWS Administrator/DevOps Engineer
Department/Group: Cloud Operations
Location: [Specify location]
Grade: L2
Role: Manage and optimize AWS infrastructure, and contribute to DevOps initiatives.
Experience: 2-4 years of relevant experience.

Roles and Responsibilities:
Manage and administer AWS services, including EC2, S3, RDS, and VPC.
Develop and maintain automation scripts for infrastructure provisioning and configuration management.
Implement monitoring and alerting solutions for AWS resources.
Collaborate with development teams to streamline CI/CD pipelines and deployment workflows.
Develop and maintain disaster recovery (DR) and business continuity (BC) plans for AWS environments.
Implement infrastructure as code (IaC) practices using tools like Terraform or AWS CloudFormation.
Conduct performance tuning and optimization of AWS resources based on workload requirements.
Design and implement backup and restore strategies for critical data stored in AWS services.
Lead and participate in periodic reviews and audits of AWS environments to ensure compliance with organizational standards and regulatory requirements.

Qualifications and Education Requirements:
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
Hands-on experience with AWS services and infrastructure management.
Proficiency in scripting languages like Python, Bash, or PowerShell.

Preferred Skills:
AWS Certified Solutions Architect or AWS Certified DevOps Engineer certification.
Experience with infrastructure as code (IaC) tools like Terraform or CloudFormation.
Familiarity with container orchestration platforms like Kubernetes.
